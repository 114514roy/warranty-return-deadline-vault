What It Would Do

Deadline-first records

Track return windows, warranty expiries, and service contract dates for high-value purchases.

Proof in one place

Keep receipt references, photos, serial numbers, model details, and support notes together.

Missing-proof checklist

Flag missing receipt, serial number, photo, return date, or warranty note before contacting support.

Proof packet export

Export a local packet for a seller, retailer, manufacturer, or your own records.

Who This Is For

  • People buying electronics, appliances, cameras, tools, bikes, or furniture.
  • Anyone who has missed a return window or struggled to find warranty proof.
  • Households that want purchase records without a cloud account or retailer lock-in.

Boundary

This concept organizes user-owned records. It does not determine warranty eligibility, enforce refunds, provide legal advice, or connect to retailers or manufacturers.
