Warranty Return Vault Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-15

Warranty Return Vault is local-first. Purchase records, receipt references, serial notes, deadlines, warranty notes, support notes, and generated proof packet content are stored on the user's device.

Data Collection

The app does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, contact lists, location data, health data, financial account credentials, retailer accounts, or manufacturer account credentials.

Purchases

The lifetime unlock is handled through Apple's in-app purchase system. The app does not receive or store payment card details.
