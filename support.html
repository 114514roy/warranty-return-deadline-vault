Warranty Return Vault Support

Warranty Return Vault helps keep receipt proof, serial numbers, return dates, warranty dates, and support notes together for high-value purchases.

Common Tasks

  • Create a purchase record with item, merchant, amount, model, and serial notes.
  • Add return, warranty, and service-plan deadlines.
  • Review the missing-proof checklist before contacting support.
  • Export a local proof packet when you need a record outside the app.
  • Restore the lifetime unlock from the purchase screen.
